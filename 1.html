About Us

Kashiyatra, the annual IIT(BHU), Varanasi's Socio-Cultural Festival, is a three-day fiesta that aims to captivate everyone with its enchanting literary, musical, and artistic events. It is the largest North Indian cultural festival.

IIT(BHU) is one of the largest residential universities in Asia. It is situated in the holy city of Varanasi, the Eternal City, home to a plethora of traditions and an ecstatic mix of cultures from across our country. IIT(BHU) recently marked its centenary, making this year's Kashiyatra even more special.

Theme

Cadence Kaleidoscope: Old Soul, New Groove

This year’s theme celebrates the harmonious blend of our rich past cultures and traditions with modern-age practices. It honors the fusion of classic and futuristic elements to create a vibrant and unique funfair experience. Embracing the vast diversity of cultures and customs, it also celebrates the potential of the future, offering a truly unique and immersive experience.
